About Me

Hello World!

My name is Brian Gabot. I am a developer currently seeking a junior front-end developer position.

I’ve always been fascinated with computers. Ever since I got my first computer in 2006, I always wondered how the websites were created. Although I was intrigued with website creation, I never considered making a career out of it because I assumed you had to be a mathematical genius to succeed in the field. Instead, I decided to study business management in college because I thought that it would be the best career based on my skillset.

However, after working on a website project for a startup I was working for at the time, I realized where my true passion was. My true passion was to create things people would find useful. After playing around with some javascript and html on the web, I was intrigued by what you can create with lines of code. After a few months of self-taught programming, I decided to transition into a web development career.

While I'm not in front of my computer developing or illustrating, I like to play table-top or video games.
